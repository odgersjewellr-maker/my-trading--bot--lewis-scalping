# REVIVEЯ — First 10 TikTok Scripts

*All filmable on a phone, natural light, no editing beyond cuts and text
overlays. Each script: hook (first 1.5s), shot list, overlay text, and
caption. Post 1–2 per day, rotate products, and re-film any hook that breaks
20k views with new footage.*

**Rules for every video:**
- Start mid-action. Never open with a logo, an intro, or "hey guys."
- Use trending audio at low volume under a voiceover, or no voiceover at all.
- Experiential language only — "feels incredible", never "fixes/treats/heals".
- CTA is soft until the account has traction: "link in bio" or nothing at all.
  Hard-sell CTAs on a small account kill reach.
- Handle: @reviverco · bio line: "Recovery tools that read both ways ↔ Ships from AU"

## RING-1 · "Deep dig" demo (the banker — film this first)

- **Hook (text on screen):** "This gets spots a foam roller physically can't"
- **Shot 1 (0–1.5s):** Close-up: calf sliding through the ring, mid-roll. No lead-in.
- **Shot 2 (1.5–8s):** Slow roll down the calf, ring flexing. Audible exhale.
- **Shot 3 (8–15s):** Second body part — forearm. Overlay: "desk workers: this one's yours"
- **Shot 4 (15–20s):** Hold ring up to camera, rotate once. Overlay: "no batteries. no noise. just pressure."
- **Caption:** the foam roller is jealous · #recoverytools #musclerecovery #gymtok #deskjob #fyp

## RING-2 · POV skeptic

- **Hook (voiceover):** "I thought this ring thing was a gimmick."
- **Shot 1:** Ring tossed onto a bench/mat, slightly dismissive.
- **Shot 2:** "Then leg day happened." — walking downstairs badly (comedy beat).
- **Shot 3:** Using it on quads, face changes. Overlay: "oh. OH."
- **Shot 4:** Ring now sitting on the bedside table. Overlay: "it lives here now"
- **Caption:** day 9 of being wrong about it · #legday #recovery #fitnesstok

## RING-3 · Routine slot

- **Hook (text):** "3 minutes after every run. Non-negotiable."
- **Shots:** Running shoes off at the door → timer on phone (3:00) → calves
  through the ring while still in running gear → timer hits 0:00 → walking
  off normally. No voiceover, one continuous vibe, trending chill audio.
- **Caption:** the run is optional. this isn't · #runningtok #runrecovery #5k

## RING-4 · ASMR / satisfying

- **Hook:** Extreme close-up of the ring flexing around a forearm, slow.
- **Shots:** 15 seconds of slow rolls, three body parts, three angles. Text
  overlay only at the end: "REVIVEЯ" + "it reads both ways"
- **Note:** This is the brand-builder. Some of these flop, some do 500k.
  Costs nothing to film alongside RING-1.
- **Caption:** sound on · #asmr #satisfying #recoverytools

## BALLS-1 · The wall trick

- **Hook (voiceover):** "Your shoulder blades are begging for this."
- **Shot 1:** Ball between back and wall, mid-movement, face already reacting.
- **Shot 2:** Demo of the movement — knees bending, ball tracing the shoulder blade.
- **Shot 3:** Peanut ball on the floor version. Overlay: "floor mode for the brave"
- **Caption:** free physio-ish feeling at home (not physio. better company) · #deskworker #shoulderpain? — ❌ *don't use "pain" hashtags — swap to* #deskstretch #officelife #recoverytools

## BALLS-2 · Handbag reveal

- **Hook (text):** "Things in my gym bag that actually get used"
- **Shots:** Bag unzip → phone, keys, then the ball duo → quick cut of each in
  use (3s each) → back in the bag. Fast cuts, upbeat audio.
- **Caption:** the $29 items outworking my $400 shoes · #gymbag #gymtok #whatsinmybag

## STRAP-1 · The hamstring test

- **Hook (voiceover):** "Can you touch your toes? Be honest."
- **Shot 1:** Failing a toe-touch, badly. Comedy beat.
- **Shot 2:** Loop strap on foot, easing deeper one loop at a time. Overlay
  counts: "loop 4… loop 5… loop 6"
- **Shot 3:** Re-test toe-touch — visibly closer. Overlay: "day 1. imagine day 30."
- **Caption:** the loops are cheat codes · #flexibility #stretching #hamstrings

## ICE-1 · The 6am face

- **Hook (text):** "POV: 6am and your face knows it"
- **Shots:** Freezer opens (roller lives next to the peas) → first roll across
  cheek, sharp inhale → slow rolls, visibly waking up → final look at camera,
  small nod. Morning light, no voiceover.
- **Caption:** cold plunge for people who won't cold plunge · #morningroutine #selfcaretok #iceroller

## KIT-1 · Unboxing + routine
