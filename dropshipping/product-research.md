### 1. Compression packing cubes (dual-zipper) — score 7/7 ⭐

- **Where found:** Copyfy July 2026 list; peak travel season
- **Supplier cost:** ~£5–8/set · **Selling price:** ~£22–25/set (~3x)
- **Checklist:** ✅ solves problem (luggage space) · ✅ not in local shops ·
  ✅ 3x markup · ✅ in cost range · ✅ flat/light/no battery · ✅ no trademark ·
  ✅ trending (peak travel month)
- **Why it works:** great before/after demo video ("fit 2 weeks in a carry-on"),
  ships flat so shipping is cheap, and travel runs into autumn city-break
  season — not a summer-only product.
- **Verdict: TEST — top pick.**

### 2. Fascia ring / deep-tissue recovery ring — score 7/7 ⭐

- **Where found:** Sell The Trend July 2026 (source ~£13, retail ~£38, ~£25 gross)
- **Supplier cost:** ~£13 · **Selling price:** ~£38–40 (~3x)
- **Checklist:** ✅ problem (muscle pain/recovery) · ✅ not in local shops ·
  ✅ 3x · ✅ in range · ✅ solid/no battery · ✅ no trademark · ✅ FitTok traction
- **Why it works:** fitness recovery is evergreen, the demo is satisfying to
  watch, and the higher price point leaves real room for ad costs later.
- **Verdict: TEST — top pick.**

### 3. Pet grooming/bath brush (de-shedding) — score 7/7 ⭐

- **Where found:** Sell The Trend pet niche (top performers averaging ~67%
  margin); #PetTok
- **Supplier cost:** ~£3–5 · **Selling price:** ~£13–15 (~3x)
- **Checklist:** ✅ problem (shedding everywhere) · ✅ mostly online product ·
  ✅ 3x · ✅ in range · ✅ light/no battery · ✅ no trademark · ✅ PetTok is huge
- **Why it works:** pet owners are impulse buyers, content is easy (any dog
  enjoying a brush goes viral), fully evergreen. Low price = cheap to test.
- **Verdict: TEST — top pick.**
